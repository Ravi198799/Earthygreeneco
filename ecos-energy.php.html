Why Switch to Solar Power in Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka has long been recognized as one of the most expensive energy markets in the world.

Rising electricity costs have left many homeowners and businesses grappling with hefty bills.

However, the adoption of solar power presents a compelling solution.

Here's why you should make the switch to solar energy with Eco's Solar:

Significant Cost Savings

By harnessing the power of the sun, you can significantly reduce or even eliminate your electricity bills. Imagine enjoying the benefits of electricity without the burden of high monthly expenses.

Uninterrupted Power Supply

Solar power provides you with a reliable source of electricity, ensuring you have an uninterrupted power supply, even during grid outages. This reliability is especially crucial for businesses and homes that rely on a consistent energy supply.

Rapid Payback Period

Investing in solar power is not only environmentally friendly but financially savvy as well. In Sri Lanka, the average payback period for a solar power system is just five years. After this period, you'll not only be covering your energy needs but also earning money from the energy your roof generates.

Solar Electricity Solutions

Eco's Solar offers various solar electricity solutions tailored to your specific needs

Net Metering

  • Ideal for high electricity bills and limited roof space.
  • Zero your bill if you generate as much electricity as you consume.
  • Pay only for the energy you consume minus what your solar panels generate.
  • Surplus energy rolls forward and reduces your future bills.

Net Accounting

  • Perfect for low electricity bills and ample roof space.
  • Eliminate your bill by generating as much electricity as you consume.
  • Earn LKR 37/- per unit for excess electricity you produce.

Net Plus

  • Suited for industrial electricity consumers with large roof areas.
  • Earn LKR 37/- per unit for the energy you generate.
  • Continue paying your electricity bill as usual.

Components of On-Grid Solar PV System

Solar Panels

Capture sunlight during daylight hours.

Inverter

Converts DC electricity from the panels into AC electricity.

Meter

Diverts AC electricity to the national grid and your home.

Monitoring

Track production and performance via smartphone or computer.

Components of Hybrid Solar PV System

Solar Panels

Convert sunlight into DC electricity.

Inverter

Charges the battery and converts DC to AC.

Battery

Stores excess energy for later use.

Meter

Channels AC electricity to the grid and your home.

Backup Power

During grid failures, the battery powers your home.

Monitoring

Monitor production and system performance.

    Eco's Solar Hot Water System

    Eco's Solar Hot Water System is at the forefront of innovative, eco-friendly heating solutions. Our advanced design features an evacuated tube solar collector, offering an efficient and environmentally conscious way to heat water. Here's how it works:

    HOW IT WORKS

    Solar Collector

    The heart of our system, the evacuated tube solar collector, captures the sun's thermal energy with its unique design.

    Heat Transfer

    A circulator is employed to transfer the captured heat into water by circulating it through the solar collector.

    Water Storage

    The heated water is then stored in a tank, where it continues to be warmed throughout the day.

    On-Demand Hot Water

    With our system, you can access hot water for your home whenever you need it, without the wait time of traditional boilers.

      Bio Gas

      Inspired by methods used in Germany, India, Sweden, and various other countries, Sri Lankan Scientist Srimal Tissera has invented a biogas-producing system suitable for Sri Lankan homes and industries.

      Mr. Srimal Tissera holds a patent for his invention and received the Certificate of Merit in the field of 'Public Welfare' at the Presidential Awards of 2010 for his innovative compact biogas plant.

      The Biogas System

      The bio-gas plant is designed with an artificial stomach, similar to a human stomach.

      It consists of a pipe fixed by the side of the unit, serving as a feeder for waste input. The waste is then transported to the top tank, where it undergoes digestion.

      After digestion, it travels down to the center of the tank and begins fermentation, resulting in the production of gas. An outlet is integrated into the unit for collecting the gas.

      Innovative Activation and Control

      Mr. Srimal Tissera, the inventor, developed a unique method using traditional herbal culture to activate and control the growth of methane-producing bacteria within the biogas system.

      He also produced different cultures tailored to different raw materials, enabling the production of biogas from various sources such as food waste, greenery, water hyacinth, fat, and oil.

      Our Mission

      To bring prosperity to our motherland through the Eco's Energy concept.

      Our Vision

      This concept is highly beneficial to the nation, as biogas technology is invaluable and long-lasting, producing clean fuel and high-quality organic fertilizer from readily available organic waste.

      HOW IT WORKS

      The digestion unit of the tank ferments organic waste, with biogas accumulating in the upper unit.

      Organic kitchen waste, mixed with water, is deposited in the inlet pipe of the unit twice a day.The outlet pipe of the digestion unit discharges active liquid compost manure.

      When green gas is generated, the upper unit is lifted, and by fixing a gas tube to the gas outlet, green gas can be channeled out.

      The green gas unit produces energy for 1-7 hours daily, depending on the tank's size.

      Benefits

      • 95% of the investment (material cost) in this system represents savings, with increasing value over time.
      • The prefabricated system saves space and installation time.
      • The entire biogas system is portable and can be relocated as needed.
